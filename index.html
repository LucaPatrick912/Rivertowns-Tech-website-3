Technology Education for the Community of Hastings

Bridging the Digital Gap for Seniors

Founders: Luca & Levi

Our Mission

We are committed to bringing technology to all. Our goal is to provide seniors in our community with the knowledge and confidence to effectively use technology through hands-on training. We aim to empower older adults by making technology more accessible, intuitive, and beneficial in their daily lives.

What We Offer

  • Basic Tech Skills: Using smartphones, tablets, and computers
  • Helpful Tips & Tricks: Shortcuts, useful apps, and time-saving features
  • Troubleshooting: Diagnosing and solving common tech issues
  • Password Management: Creating, saving, and managing secure passwords
  • Photo & Video Organization: Storage and sharing across platforms
  • Email Assistance: Sending, organizing, and managing email communications
  • Calendars & Reminders: Digital tools to stay organized and on schedule
  • Texting & Social Media: Staying connected with family and friends
  • Storage Management: Freeing up space and using cloud storage
  • Online Safety: Avoiding scams, protecting data, and browsing safely

How This Impacts the Community

Technology has the power to improve quality of life. For older adults, it can reduce feelings of isolation, improve mental well-being, and provide greater access to healthcare, news, and social connections. By increasing digital literacy, we help bridge generational gaps and foster a more connected, inclusive community. Our mission strengthens not just individuals, but our town as a whole.
